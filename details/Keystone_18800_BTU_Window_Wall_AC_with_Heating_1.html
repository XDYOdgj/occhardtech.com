Keystone Window Wall AC Unit - 18800 BTU Cooling & 16000 BTU Heating

Experience seamless climate control with the Keystone window wall air conditioner. This all-in-one unit provides powerful cooling during the summer and efficient supplemental heating in the winter, making it perfect for any large space up to 1,000 square feet. Ideal for living rooms, apartments, or open areas, it ensures year-round comfort without the need for separate units.

Equipped with advanced features, the Keystone unit delivers top performance. The smart remote allows for easy temperature adjustments, maintaining your ideal environment automatically every 3 minutes.
