ACiQ-07PTC PTAC Unit: Efficient Cooling and Heating for Small Spaces

The ACiQ-07PTC PTAC unit is an ideal choice for cooling and heating small rooms up to 200-300 square feet. With its high efficiency and 13.1 EER rating, it delivers optimal comfort while minimizing energy consumption. The built-in 2.5 kW backup electric heat system ensures continuous warmth even in the coldest weather, making it perfect for year-round use.

Designed with ease of installation in mind, the ACiQ-07PTC includes a drain kit and a 7-foot power cord with a NEMA 6-15P plug. It is compatible with existing PTAC wall sleeves and grilles, allowing seamless integration into your current setup. Whether for residential or commercial use, this unit brings modern comfort and energy-saving benefits to any space.

Smart Features for Enhanced Control and Convenience

Experience the convenience of smart technology with the ACiQ-07PTC. It features a sleek LED temperature display that makes monitoring the indoor climate simple and intuitive. The unit includes two washable pre-installed air filters, improving air quality and reducing maintenance needs.

With real-time temperature monitoring and compatibility with third-party infrared sensors, you can fine-tune your environment with precision. Its built-in WiFi system and remote control allow you to manage your climate from anywhere through the ACiQ app, providing peace of mind and flexibility.
