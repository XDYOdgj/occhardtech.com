Coolblus Portable AC – Cool, Dry, and Conserve

Coolblus Portable AC offers up to 360 square feet of cooling coverage with a robust 8500 BTU capacity. This powerful air conditioner is designed to bring quick, effective cooling to your home or office, reducing temperatures to a comfortable range of 61°F to 88°F in as little as 15 minutes.

With a 3-in-1 functionality, you can switch between cooling, fan, or drying modes for tailored comfort. The smart sleep mode ensures ultra-quiet operation at just 52 dB, ideal for undisturbed sleep. The 24-hour timer gives you the flexibility to set your cooling schedule, and the included window installation kit and 1.5-meter exhaust hose make setup easy and hassle-free.

The built-in energy-efficient R32 refrigerant not only enhances cooling performance but also lowers your energy bills. An easy-to-clean mesh filter and automatic drainage system simplify maintenance, making Coolblus a reliable and low-maintenance solution. The compact and portable design, complete with 360° pulleys and built-in handles, allows you to move this unit effortlessly between rooms, ensuring continuous comfort wherever you need it.

Whether you're looking to cool your bedroom, living room, office, or any other small area, Coolblus Portable AC is the perfect choice. Its intuitive remote control offers a 33-foot range, letting you manage your cooling setup without being in close proximity. The unit also automatically restarts after a power outage, ensuring your comfort is never interrupted.

Key Features

  • Energy-efficient R32 refrigerant for optimal cooling and lower operational costs.
  • 360 sq ft cooling capacity with rapid temperature adjustment for quick results.
  • Dehumidifying up to 95 pints per day to maintain a dry and comfortable indoor environment.
  • Smart sleep mode ensures ultra-quiet performance at 52 dB for peaceful nights.
  • 24-hour timer and easy installation with included window kit and exhaust hose.
