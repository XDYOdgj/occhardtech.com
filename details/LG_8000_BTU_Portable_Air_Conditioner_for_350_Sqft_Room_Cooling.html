Stay Cool, Comfortable, and Eco-Friendly with the LG 8000 BTU Portable AC Unit

Whether it's a hot summer day or simply the need for a refreshed space, the LG 8000 BTU portable air conditioner is the perfect solution. Designed to cool up to 350 square feet, it's ideal for bedrooms, basements, and small offices. Its powerful yet quiet operation ensures that you can enjoy a comfortable environment without any disturbance from loud noise.

With three-in-one Cooling, Dry, and Fan modes, this AC unit adapts to your lifestyle and seasonal needs. You can easily switch between these functions to maintain the ideal temperature and humidity levels throughout your day. Plus, the included installation kit and hose make setup and storage a breeze, giving you added convenience and flexibility.

The LG ThinQ app provides remote control access, letting you adjust settings from anywhere. It also supports voice assistant compatibility with Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa, enabling hands-free operation. This unit is built with eco-friendly R32 refrigerant, which not only helps in reducing your carbon footprint but also contributes to a quieter, more energy-efficient cooling experience.
