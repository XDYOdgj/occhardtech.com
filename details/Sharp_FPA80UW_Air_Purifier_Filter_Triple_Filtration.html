Sharp FPA80UW Air Purifier Filter

Breathing clean air has never been easier with this premium Sharp air purifier filter. Featuring a powerful triple filtration system, it tackles dust, smoke, and pollen with precision and efficiency. Start with a washable pre-filter to trap larger particles, then move on to an activated carbon deodorizing filter for fresh, odour-free air, and finally, a True HEPA filter that captures 99.97% of 0.3-micron airborne pollutants.

  • Efficient Performance: With a high CADR rating of 293 for smoke, 303 for dust, and 360 for pollen, this filter is ideal for large rooms up to 2,198 sq. ft. It delivers clean air quickly and effectively.

  • Safety & Certification: Approved by ENERGY STAR and AHAM Verifide, this filter is built to meet the highest standards of performance and safety, giving you peace of mind with every use.

Whether you're looking to purify your home or office, this Sharp filter offers exceptional value. It combines powerful cleaning with user-friendly design, making it the perfect choice for anyone who values clean, healthy air.
