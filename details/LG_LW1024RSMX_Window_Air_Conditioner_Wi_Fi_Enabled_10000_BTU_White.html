LG LW1024RSMX Window Air Conditioner

Designed for optimal performance and ease of use, this air conditioner offers powerful cooling for medium to large spaces up to 450 square feet.

Efficient Cooling for Your Space

With a 10,000 BTU output, the LG LW1024RSMX delivers strong cooling power, making it ideal for rooms like your living area, bedroom, or office. Whether you're looking to stay comfortable during the hottest days or maintain a steady temperature for your family, this unit provides reliable performance.

Smart & Convenient Control

Leverage the LG ThinQ technology to control your air conditioner remotely through your smartphone or by using voice commands with Alexa and Google Assistant. This smart integration adds a modern touch to your home, ensuring you can adjust settings from anywhere with ease.

Advanced Features for Ultimate Comfort

The 4-way air deflection system ensures that cool air is distributed evenly throughout the room, providing balanced comfort. Choose from 3 cooling modes and multiple fan speeds to personalize your experience and maintain ideal temperatures.

Easy Maintenance & Eco-Friendly Design

Its slide-out, washable filter makes cleaning simple and maintenance hassle-free, promoting better indoor air quality and energy efficiency. The AC unit is also equipped with an eco-friendly R32 refrigerant, helping reduce your environmental footprint without compromising performance.

Whether you're after quiet operation, smart integration, or efficient cooling, the LG LW1024RSMX Window Air Conditioner is a smart choice for your home.
